For reviewing and learn

1. installed opencv-python package, otherwise the cv2 package will show it's not installed

2. cv2 package Handles all the complex mathematical operations required to understand what's on the screen,
和image processing选修上面是类似的, 比如cv2.cvtColor(), cv2.imread(), cv2.matchTemplate()等等

3. numpy作用是: Since images are just massive arrays of numbers (pixel color values),
 NumPy is crucial for handling them quickly. OpenCV operations are optimized to run on NumPy arrays,
  making image processing much faster than standard Python lists.

4.pyautogui就是模拟鼠标移动,点击,以及识别screenshot
